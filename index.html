About Me

My Projects

Artificial Intelligence(AI)

The goal of the RAICA curriculum is computational action with artificial intelligence for late primary, middle and secondary students. In order for students to take computational action with AI, RAICA has designed lessons that provide opportunities for students to apply computational thinking and responsible design while growing their AI fluency. We approach all of our work with a constructionist pedagogy, a belief that students learn best by constructing their own knowledge and being creative.

Robotics

Robotics for kids involves designing, building, and programming robots, blending coding, engineering, and creativity to teach STEM concepts through hands-on projects. It develops critical thinking, problem-solving, and teamwork skills as children learn from basic circuits to complex functions like obstacle avoidance. Resources range from age-specific kits like LEGO ® SPIKE™ Prime and Sphero to online classes and camps, providing engaging ways for kids to learn the fundamentals of robotics

Web Development

Web development is the process of creating, maintaining, and managing websites and web applications that run online. It combines technical and creative skills to build everything from simple static pages to complex social media platforms and e-commerce sites. Key aspects include designing user interfaces, programming website functionality, managing databases, implementing security, and optimizing for performance and search engines
